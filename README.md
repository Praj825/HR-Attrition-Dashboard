# 👥 HR Attrition Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
Analyzed **1,470 employee records** to identify attrition patterns and high-risk segments using Power BI and DAX.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI** (DAX, Power Query)
- **Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset (Kaggle)

---

## 📊 Key Insights

| # | Insight |
|---|---|
| 1 | Overall Attrition Rate: **16.12%** (237 employees) |
| 2 | **Lab Technicians** most affected — 62 attritions |
| 3 | **26–35 age group** highest risk — 116 attritions |
| 4 | Overtime employees show significantly higher attrition (127 vs 110) |
| 5 | Sales & R&D departments contribute most to attrition |

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- KPI cards — Attrition Rate, Total Employees, Active Employees
- Department-wise attrition breakdown
- Age group analysis
- Job role wise attrition
- Overtime impact visualization
- Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- **Reduce overtime** for Lab Technicians — highest burnout risk
- **Focus retention programs** on 26–35 age group
- **Review compensation** for high-attrition job roles
